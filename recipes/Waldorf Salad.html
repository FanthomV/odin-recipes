Waldorf Salad

Description

A Waldorf salad is a fruit and nut salad that features a mayonnaise dressing. It was created in 1896 by Oscar Tschirky, the maître d'hôtel at New York’s Waldorf-Astoria. It doesn’t traditionally contain meat, but chicken is often added to make it a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples — peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  • Step 2: Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt together in a serving bowl.
  • Step 3: Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
